Module 4: Advanced Practices

  • Khodam Macan Putih (White Tiger): This khodam symbolizes strength, courage, and spiritual protection. Practitioners often call upon the White Tiger for guidance in overcoming fears and facing challenges. It is believed to empower individuals with resilience and clarity, allowing them to navigate through life's obstacles with confidence. This khodam also aids in fostering leadership qualities and instilling a sense of purpose.

  • Khodam Macan Kumbang (Black Panther): Known for its stealth and agility, the Black Panther khodam assists practitioners in harnessing their personal power and charisma. This khodam helps in overcoming obstacles that may impede progress, making it particularly valuable during times of transition. Additionally, the Black Panther promotes intuition and deeper insight, guiding individuals to make wise decisions in various aspects of life.

  • Khodam Kera Putih (White Monkey): The White Monkey is associated with intelligence, creativity, and playfulness. This khodam encourages innovative thinking and helps practitioners tap into their creative potential. By fostering an environment of curiosity and adaptability, the White Monkey assists individuals in finding unique solutions to challenges. Additionally, this khodam enhances social skills and relationships, enabling better communication and understanding with others.
